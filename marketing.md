# Visual Engine for Focus Timer - Marketing & Benefits Guide

## What is Visual Engine?

**Visual Engine for Focus Timer** is an immersive web application that combines mesmerizing animated visuals with a focus timer and ambient noise generation. It's designed to help users achieve deep focus, relaxation, and mindfulness through a beautiful, customizable cosmic meditation experience.

Created by **Nick Zaleski**, this application transforms your screen into a living, breathing visual environment that adapts to your preferences and supports your productivity and wellness goals.

## Getting Started

1. **Open the Application**: Navigate to the Visual Engine website
2. **Choose Your Visual Mode**: Select from Breathing Blob, Gradient Flow, or Plasma
3. **Customize Colors**: (For Breathing Blob) Pick your favorite color or use a preset
4. **Set Your Timer**: Choose duration or use quick presets (10, 25, 60 minutes)
5. **Add Ambient Noise**: (Optional) Select white, pink, or brown noise and adjust volume
6. **Go Fullscreen**: Click "Enter Fullscreen" for maximum immersion
7. **Start Your Session**: Press "Start Timer" and begin your focused work or meditation
